🎭 TRAITORS BIRTHDAY

Player Handbook
Phase 1 · Welcome

The Game

You've been invited to a secret game. Some of you are Faithful. A few of you are Traitors. You'll play 3 games and hold Roundtables between them. Each night, the Traitors secretly murder one Faithful. At each Roundtable, the group votes to banish someone they suspect.

Win Conditions

  • Faithful win if all Traitors are banished.
  • Traitors win if any Traitor survives to the end.

🛡️ Labubus Are Shields

  • Winning a game earns you a Labubu shield.
  • Hold the Labubu to block ONE murder attempt — then it's gone.
  • One shield per game, per player.
  • Shields do NOT block roundtable banishment. You can still be voted out.
  • Team shields (Movie Challenge, Color Box) protect the whole winning team from the next murder phase.

💎 Game 1 — Hidden Diamonds

Five minutes. Find plastic diamonds hidden around the clubhouse.

  • One diamond per hand — max 2 at a time.
  • Stack them on your named coffin card.
  • You must return to your coffin before grabbing more.
  • Most diamonds wins a Labubu shield 🛡️.

📜 Rules of Engagement

  • Lie, bluff, mislead — this is a social deception game. That's the point.
  • You may NOT show your role card to anyone, ever.
  • You may NOT take photos of role cards.
  • Respect the ceremonies (close your eyes when told, no peeking).
  • Have fun. It's a game. No one is actually mad at you for winning.
Phase 2 · Roundtable

⚖️ How a Roundtable Works

  1. Discussion (~8 min): everyone sits at the roundtable. Accuse, defend, ask questions, build alliances. Be loud, be quiet, be sneaky — your call.
  2. Vote: the host calls for a vote. On the count of 3, everyone points simultaneously at the person they want to banish.
  3. Reveal: majority is banished. The host reveals their role card dramatically.
  4. Banished player becomes a Ghost.

Voting Rules

  • Simultaneous pointing — no abstaining, no changing your mind after the 3-count.
  • Ties → host picks a tiebreaker on the spot (coin flip, revote between tied players, etc.).
  • You can vote for yourself. We don't recommend it.
Phase 3 · Ghosts

👻 You're Dead. Here's What You Can Do.

1. The Ghost Vote

  • Each roundtable, ghosts meet privately and agree on ONE player to collectively vote for.
  • The host announces "The ghosts have cast their vote — they chose ___" before the living vote.
  • The ghost vote doesn't auto-banish anyone, but it heavily influences the living. Use it as propaganda.

2. Return to Life

  • Ghosts can still play the 3 games.
  • If a Ghost is on a winning team, that ghost may swap places with a chosen living player — ghost comes back to life, living player becomes a ghost.
  • Your role stays the same as it was when you died.

3. Shield Nullification

  • If a ghost returns to life by targeting a shielded player, the shield is consumed and the swap still happens.

Ghost Etiquette

  • No talking to living players about the game during roundtables (hang out and drink, though).
  • No revealing who murdered whom — you're dead, you don't know.
  • Your role card is still secret.
Phase 4 · Movie Challenge

🎬 Game 2 — Movie Challenge

10–15 minutes. Three teams of ~3, each team split into Actors and Guessers in separate rooms.

How to Play

  • Actors pick a movie from their poster board.
  • Act it out in ONE photo and send it to your Guessers.
  • Guessers walk around and grab the matching title strip taped to the wall.
  • Bring the strip back as proof. Actors send the next movie photo once the previous is guessed.

Scoring

  • 1 point per correct guess.
  • Once a movie is guessed correctly by ANY team, it's locked out for everyone.
  • No two teams can act out the same movie — first come first served.

Prize

🛡️ Team Shield — the whole winning team is protected from the next murder. Team members can still be banished by roundtable vote.

Phase 5 · Color Box

🎨 Game 3 — Color Box (Mastermind)

Six secret colors in a hidden order inside the box. Crack the code.

How to Play

  • Three teams take turns.
  • On each turn: arrange the 6 colors on top of the box in your guessed order → say "LOCK IN".
  • The host announces "X correct positions" — only exact-spot matches count.
  • Next team's turn. Keep cycling until one team cracks all 6, or ~2:30 per team rotation elapses.

How to Win

  • First team to lock in all 6 colors correctly wins.
  • Time out? Closest guess (highest X) wins — tiebreaker by who achieved it first.

Prize

🛡️ Team Shield — same as Movie Challenge (whole team protected from next murder, still banishable).

Phase 6 · Endgame

⚖️ Final Roundtable

  • Minimum ONE banishment is required.
  • After each banishment, the host asks: "Keep going or stop?" Majority decides.
  • When the group votes to stop (or only 2 players remain), win conditions are checked.

🏆 Win Conditions

  • Faithful win if all Traitors are banished.
  • Traitors win if any Traitor survives to the end.
